WATER INTAKE

Water intake is crucial for maintaining good health, as water plays a vital role in various bodily functions. The amount of water a person needs can vary based on factors such as age, sex, weight, physical activity level, and climate. However, a general guideline is to aim for about 8 glasses of water per day, which is roughly 64 ounces or about 2 liters.
