Frequently Asked Questions

"75 Hard" is a popular fitness and mental toughness challenge created by entrepreneur Andy Frisella. Participants commit to a set of daily tasks for 75 consecutive days, with the goal of building discipline and resilience

1. What is the 75 Hard challenge?

The 75 Hard challenge is a mental toughness and fitness challenge created by Andy Frisella. It involves completing a set of daily tasks for 75 consecutive days.

2. What are the daily tasks in the 75 Hard challenge?

The core tasks include:= Follow a strict diet without cheat meals or alcohol. Exercise twice a day for at least 45 minutes each session. Read 10 pages of a non-fiction book. Drink one gallon (about 3.8 liters) of water per day. Take a progress picture each day.

3. Can I modify the tasks to suit my fitness level or schedule?

No, the challenge is meant to be completed exactly as outlined. Modifying the tasks is discouraged, as the goal is to build mental toughness through strict adherence to the program.

4. What happens if I miss a task or break a rule during the challenge?

If you miss any of the daily tasks or break a rule, you must start over from day one. The challenge is designed to be a strict test of discipline.

5. Is the 75 Hard challenge suitable for everyone?

The challenge is intense and may not be suitable for everyone. It's recommended to consult with a healthcare professional before starting, especially if you have pre-existing health conditions.
